Fathoms Below

Did you know that 80% of the ocean is unexplored? A group of oceanographers have set out to map and observe the unknown, by taking a submarine to the bottom of the Mariana Trench.

However, the ocean is unforgiving. Some things the crew cannot control. Such as hydrostatic pressure that can be up to 1,100 times stronger than pressure at sea level. Or the seemingly fictitious creatures that call this barren and hostile region their home. The only thing that they can do is prepare for the unknown and hope for the best.
